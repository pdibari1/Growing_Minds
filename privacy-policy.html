2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when filling out our story intake form:

  • Your email address
  • Your child's first name, age, and gender
  • Your child's physical description (hair color, eye color)
  • Your child's personality traits, interests, and hometown
  • Names of companions, siblings, or pets you choose to include
  • Any additional details you voluntarily provide in the customization field
  • Your shipping address (collected by Stripe at checkout)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never store card details)

We also collect limited technical data automatically, including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited, via Google Analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate your personalized story book
  • Deliver your digital story by email
  • Fulfill and ship your printed book order
  • Respond to customer service inquiries
  • Send you occasional updates about Growing Minds (you may unsubscribe at any time)
  • Improve our service through analytics

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Growing Minds is a service for parents and guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13. All information about children is provided by a parent or guardian on their behalf. By using our service, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old and are providing information about your child with full parental consent.

The information you provide about your child (name, age, appearance, interests) is used solely to generate their personalized story. We do not share this information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We share your data only with trusted service providers necessary to operate our business:

  • Stripe — payment processing and shipping address collection
  • Resend — email delivery of your story
  • Lulu Direct — printing and fulfillment of physical books
  • Anthropic — AI story generation (no personal data is retained by Anthropic beyond what is needed to process the request)
  • Google Analytics — anonymous website usage analytics
  • Airtable — internal customer record keeping

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address and order information for as long as necessary to fulfill your order and provide customer support. Story content is temporarily stored during generation and deleted within 2 hours of your order being fulfilled. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@growingminds.io.

7. Cookies

We use Google Analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our website. These cookies collect anonymous data such as pages visited and time spent on site. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@growingminds.io.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure third-party payment processing via Stripe. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

About the Book

What exactly is included in my order?
You get two things: an instant digital copy of the full story emailed to you right after purchase, and a professionally printed hardcover book shipped to your door within 13–15 business days. The digital version arrives in minutes — perfect for reading together that same night.
How long is the story?
Every story is a full 30-chapter novel — approximately 12,000 words. That's the length of a real published children's book. It's designed to be read together over many nights, one chapter at a time, or to be read by older kiddos by themselves. Think of it as your child's very own bedtime series.
What ages is this for?
Growing Minds stories are written for children aged 3–13. The writing style, vocabulary, and themes are automatically adjusted based on your child's age — from warm, lyrical read-aloud prose for toddlers to rich middle-grade storytelling for tweens. It's always written to be enjoyed together as a family.
How is the story personalized?
We weave in your child's name, appearance (hair color, eye color), personality traits, favorite things, their hometown, their best friend or sibling, and a milestone they're currently working through — like starting school, learning to ride a bike, or making new friends. These details appear naturally throughout the story, not just bolted on. Every book is genuinely one of a kind.

Ordering & Delivery

When will I get the digital version?
Your personalized story is written by AI immediately after purchase and emailed to you — typically within 10–15 minutes. You'll receive the complete 30-chapter story in a beautifully formatted email, ready to read that same evening.
How long does the printed book take?
Printed books are professionally produced and shipped within 13–15 business days to US addresses. We currently ship within the United States only.
Do you ship internationally?
Not yet — we currently ship printed books within the United States only. The digital story is delivered by email and available instantly anywhere in the world. International shipping is on our roadmap!

Payment & Refunds

Why can't I get a refund?
Because every story is written uniquely for your child the moment you order — it can't be resold or reused. Like a commissioned piece of art, the work is done specifically for you. That said, if something goes wrong on our end (the email doesn't arrive, the book has a printing issue), we'll always make it right. Just email us at [email protected].
Do you offer discount codes?
Yes! If you have a discount code, enter it on the story preview page before checkout. Codes are occasionally shared through our newsletter and social media — follow us to stay in the loop.

The Story Itself

Can I preview the story before buying?
Yes — fill out the form and we'll generate a free preview of the opening of your child's story. You'll see exactly how their name, personality, and world are woven into the narrative before you decide to purchase.
Can I request changes to the story?
The story is generated fresh from the details you provide, so the best way to shape it is through the intake form — the more detail you give, the richer the story. If you're unhappy with how something came out, email us at [email protected] and we'll do our best to help.
What if I want to order for multiple children?
Just place a separate order for each child — each story is completely unique. If you're ordering for more than three children, email us at [email protected] and we may be able to offer a bundle discount.
